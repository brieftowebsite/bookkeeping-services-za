SARS support

Organised support for SARS and compliance admin.

Practical help keeping the paperwork and bookkeeping records needed for tax and compliance admin easier to manage.

Admin support can include

  • Keeping bookkeeping documents organised
  • Preparing records for accountant or tax practitioner review
  • Helping track missing documents
  • Supporting VAT, PAYE or general compliance admin routines where applicable
